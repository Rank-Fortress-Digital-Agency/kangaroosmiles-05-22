Make sure your child is brushing their teeth correctly.

The first step to keeping your child’s teeth healthy is to make sure they are brushing correctly. Teach them to brush gently in a circular motion and avoid scrubbing back and forth, which can damage the enamel of their teeth. They should also be sure to brush for two minutes at least twice a day.

If your child is still too young to brush their teeth, you should brush for them. You can use a pea-sized amount of toothpaste on their toothbrush, and be sure to brush all surfaces of their teeth, including the front, back, and chewing surfaces.
